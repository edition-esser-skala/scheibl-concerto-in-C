\chapter*{Critical Report.}

This edition bases upon a copy (Mus. Ms. 15) in the Knjižnica Ivana Potrča Ptuj. The timpani part has been written by Johannes Dandler.

In general, this edition closely follows the manuscript. Any changes that were introduced by the editor are indicated by italic type (dynamics and directions), parentheses (expressive marks) or dashes (slurs and ties). Accidentals are used according to modern conventions.


This edition has been compiled and checked with utmost diligence. Nevertheless, errors and mistakes cannot be totally excluded.
